  8. | Lt
  9. | Gte
  10. | Lte

  12. | NEq

  1. | PAny
  2. | PVar of id
  3. | PTuple of pattern * pattern * pattern list
  4. | PList of pattern list
  5. | PConst of const
  6. | POption of pattern option

  1. | EConst of const
  2. | EVar of id
  3. | EUnary of op_un * expression

  8. | EIf of expression * expression * expression option
  9. | EFun of pattern * expression
  10. | EApply of expression * expression
  11. | ELet of rec_flag * value_binding * value_binding list * expression
